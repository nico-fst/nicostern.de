A custom wedding website developed for a private client (can't really say no to a cousin who needs a Wedsite, right?).

[Dabstar-Wedding.de](https://dabstar-wedding.de) allows guests to view important information in multiple languages and saves their RSVP to the wedding. The responsive design is inspired by Apple's new, rounded Liquid Glass design language.

The project evolved through multiple feedback cycles, resulting in multilingual support and a range of client-driven design refinements.
