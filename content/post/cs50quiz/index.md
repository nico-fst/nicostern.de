After passing my A-levels, I missed working on projects and having coding targets. Therefore, I prepared for my Bachelor in Computer Science at [University of Bonn](https://www.uni-bonn.de/de) by completing the HarvardX Course "CS50's Introduction to Computer Science". CS50 Quiz evolved as my self-chosen final project:

Using the knowledge gained throughout the ten weeks of CS50x, I explored Google Firebase as a cloud-based storage solution for content (questions about the CS50x course) delivered to the Swift app.
