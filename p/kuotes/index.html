Kuotes

Remember highlights from your favourite books - directly on your homescreen

Nov 13, 2025 1 minute read

Me and my friends searched for a simple solution to recall certain key quotes from our favourite books since…

“Your mind is for having ideas, not for holding them.”
~ David Allen, Getting Things Done

So I developed Kuotes, an app that enables users to view their highlights at a glance directly form their Home Screen through widgets.
